Common questions

Where do my files go when I use Local PDF?
Nowhere but your own device. Every tool works on your file right on your device and hands the result straight back to you — your PDFs are never uploaded, and Local PDF doesn't keep a copy or a history. See the Privacy Policy for the full detail.

Does the OCR ("Scan to text") send my scans somewhere to be read?
No. The recognition runs entirely on your device. The first time you use OCR, a one-time ~13 MB engine and English language model download from Local PDF's own site and are cached for offline use — after that it works with no network at all, and your scanned pages never leave your device.

Does the OCR ("Scan to text") send my scans somewhere to be read?

Will my work sync to my other devices?
No — by design. Local PDF has no account and no cloud, and it doesn't store your documents at all. To move a result to another device, just save it and share it over (AirDrop, email, etc.).

Is there a paid plan?
Local PDF is free to use, with an optional Pro unlock: a one-time $9.99 purchase (not a subscription). Pro adds advanced compression levels (the Light and Maximum options in Compress — the default Recommended level stays free), batch processing — running Compress or PDF → Images across many files at once and getting them back as a single ZIP — plus a “Download all as ZIP” option when splitting a PDF into separate pages or exporting a PDF as page images. Every other tool is free and unlimited. Checkout is handled securely by Stripe (via RevenueCat) — the processor only ever sees the email for your receipt and your payment method, never your files. Pro comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee: if it's not for you, email support@localpdfapp.com within 30 days for a full refund. Once approved, your refund takes about 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

How do I get Pro back in a new browser?
When you unlock Pro, you're given a one-time restore code (it looks like LPDF-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX) and a license card you can save. Since there are no accounts, that code is how you re-unlock Pro in another browser — just keep it somewhere safe, along with your Stripe receipt email as proof of purchase. The iPhone and iPad app sells Pro separately through the App Store. Lost your code? Email support@localpdfapp.com and we'll help.

How do I get Pro back on a new device?
Your Pro purchase lives with your Apple Account. On a new iPhone or iPad, just tap Restore Purchases in the app and Pro comes right back — your Apple receipt is your proof of purchase. Bought on the web? Web and App Store purchases are separate — your restore code works in your browser. Questions? Email support@localpdfapp.com and we'll help.

Does Local PDF work offline?
Yes, once loaded. There's no server it depends on for day-to-day use. (Buying or restoring Pro — and the one-time OCR engine download — are the only things that need a connection.)
